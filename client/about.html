Help & more info

The service is designed to be self-explanatory: on the home page you can perform two separate actions, viz., find the n-th prime number, and obtain the prime factorisation of a number. If you want to know more about the underlying mathematical concept, consult the articles on prime numbers and on prime factorisation.

If you want to know more about the underlying method—it is slightly tweaked trial division.
